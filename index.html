Timeline

My timeline here.

  • 1993

    Where all things began

    I was born in a town with an ocean view.
    Winds, sea, and sun were my friends.
    I spent 18 years there.

  • 2011

    Freshman

    I started studying at Ho Chi Minh City University of Technology.
    A new place - Ho Chi Minh City, new things, new friends.
    Learning, joining clubs, getting mindset, knowledge, preparing for the future.

  • 2015

    Start Working

    I started working at Global Cybersoft (now it has changed its name to Hitachi Vantara).
    During this time, I was in the last semester of university and needed to work on my thesis.
    I worked on my thesis and at the company simultaneously.

  • 2016

    Graduation

    I graduated.
    No strings attached from university now.
    Now, I need to learn things from life.

  • 2018

    First Time Oversea

    I worked for 3 months in Fukuoka.
    Live alone in Japan.
    Being the only Vietnamese person in the working room.
    The most unforgettable period of my life.

  • 2019

    Oversea again

    This time, I went to Yokohama.
    COVID incoming this time.

  • 2021

    2nd year of covid

    I worked from home because of the pandemic.
    Still working in the automotive domain.
    I have been working in this domain since late 2018.

  • 2022

    Goodbye to my First Company

    I left Hitachi after six years. It was my first company, and I thought it would be my lifelong company.
    However, COVID-19 changed something in workstyle, and I don't want to work fully remote anymore.
    This company is too accommodating for employees; colleagues want to work fully remote, so the company allows them to do so.
    I don't want my colleagues to change the things they like for me, so I left.
    It feels like I just ended a relationship.

  • 2022

    Starting a New Chapter

    After one week vacation, I have a new beginning at Hella.
    This is the first time I've changed companies.
    I hope it will be the last.

  • 2024

    New Workplace

    My company moved to a brand-new office with one of the best views of Ho Chi Minh City.

  • I'm
    Still
    Going
